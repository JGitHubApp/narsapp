Frequently Asked Questions

Does NARS have job placement?

one list of lowercase keywords
No, NARS is a college credit training program and does not offer job placement services. NARS does assist students with resume’ writing and interview skills. Many NARS students go on to work at Class 1 and short line railroads after their NARS training.
+

Will FAFSA pay for the fast-track railroad certificate programs?

two
The short answer to this question is “no.” FAFSA is for degree-seeking programs only; the NARS program is a fast-track certificate program. However, if you are interested in earning an A.A.S. w/Conductor Option degree and are eligible for FAFSA, it will cover all your tuition and fees for the entire program. You must complete the FAFSA application in order to know how much and what kind of funding is available to you.

Contact Robert Murphy, JCCC counselor, rmurphy@jccc.edu, 913-469-8500 x4512, with questions about the A.A.S. w/Conductor Option. For more information on financial aid, go to the JCCC Financial Aid office webpage.
